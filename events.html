Music Café for people living with dementia and their Carers

We are working in partnership with Manchester Camerata to provide muic cafés in our community.

Music therapy has been shown to reduce the need for medication in 67% of those living with dementia. During these sessions, we give participants the opportunity to find new ways to communicate through the power of music.

Free to attend with tea / coffee / biscuits - Make new friends - No mucical experience required.
