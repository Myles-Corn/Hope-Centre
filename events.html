Music Café for people living with dementia and their Carers

We are working in partnership with Manchester Camerata to provide muic cafés in our community.

Music therapy has been shown to reduce the need for medication in 67% of those living with dementia. During these sessions, we give participants the opportunity to find new ways to communicate through the power of music.

Free to attend with tea / coffee / biscuits - Make new friends - No mucical experience required.

Prayer Stop

This is a new & exciting initiative of The Hope Centre, where we are gathering the churches of Macclesfield together to create a 'Prayer Stop' in the centre of our town ahead of Christmas.

In unity, we will be supporting our community practically & prayerfully, aware that many face challenging times.

Christmas can be a wonderful time for some people but for many it is stressful, painful & often very lonely. Prayer Stop will be held in the Grosvenor Centre 10th- 29th Nov & will provide everyone in our community the opportunity to stop & chat, find out about community events & church services that will be available to attend during the run up to Christmas and beyond. There will be volunteers available throughout the sessions to listen, chat and to also offer prayer to anyone who comes in.

A 'Prayer Tree' will be available for anyone wishing to write down their prayers & hang them on the tree as an expression of their thoughts & feelings.

Unfortunately, we have had to postpone Prayer Stop until 2026. Keep an eye out for more updates!
