Chatty Café

Looking for a friendly face and a good chat? Come and join us at Chatty Café, a welcoming space to meet new people, or simply enjoy a conversation over a cuppa.

The Chatty Café Scheme is an incredible initiative that encourages people to connect. It's all about bringing people together, reducing loneliness, and creating welcoming spaces for anyone who fancies a chat - whether you're a regular or just passing by.

Every Friday | 14:00 - 15:30 but feel feel free to join earlier!
