Volunteer with us

We're always excited to welcome new volunteers to our team!

Whether you have a few hours to spare each week or are looking for a bigger commitment, there are many ways to get involved.

Our volunteers help with everything from event support and facilitating groups to serving in the cafe and making barista-style coffees. No matter your background, your time and energy can make a real difference.

By joining our volunteer team, you'll connect with like-minded people, gain valuable experience, and play a vital role in our vision to impact our community.

Volunteering with us is great fun - we enjoy laughing together as we work alongside each other and see the real impact we create together. It is a chance to do good, feel good, and have fun along the way!
