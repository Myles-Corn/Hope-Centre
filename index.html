Where Community Meets Coffee

A warm and welcoming community hub and café on the high street, seeking to connect the community, combat isolation and loneliness, and promote health and wellbeing.

We are a Christian charity putting faith into action by serving our community, with a vision to meet people at their point of need. We provide support, connection, and hope to 'live life to the full'.

Join Our Volunteer Team

Looking to make a difference? Start right here at The Hope Centre!

The Hope Centre is more than just a place, it's a community of people coming together to support, uplift, and grow with one another.

Whether it's making barista style coffee, supporting wellbeing programmes, or offering a friendly face to someone in need, your time can make a real impact.

Café Operations

Our community café, The Well, is a friendly and relaxed space where people can connect, recharge, and feel at home.

Whether you're serving coffee and cake, or offering a kind smile, your time helps create a space full of hope, dignity, and belonging.
  • Preparing and serving drinks / snacks
  • Light cleaning and tidying
  • Supporting events and activities

Kitchen Team

Do you enjoy preparing food that brings people together?

Whether it's helping with prep, cooking, or cleanup, every role in the kitchen helps nourish both body and spirit.
  • Training and support provided
  • Learn from experienced volunteers
  • Develop valuable skills

Events & Programs

Our events aren't just about activities - they're about people, and many of our sessions are lead by volunteers who bring their talents, time, and care to make it all happen.

From singalongs to craft sessions, you can help to create spaces where everyone feels welcome.
  • Project-based commitments
  • Great for building event planning skills
  • Make a direct community impact
